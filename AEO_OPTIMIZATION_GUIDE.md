# AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Guide

## What is AEO?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing content for AI-powered search engines and language models, including:

- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Perplexity** (AI-powered search)
- **Google AI Overviews** (formerly SGE)
- **Bing Copilot** (Microsoft)
- **Other LLM-powered search engines**

## Why AEO Matters

### Traditional SEO vs. AEO

**Traditional SEO:**

- Optimizes for Google's algorithm
- Focuses on keywords and backlinks
- Goal: Rank in top 10 results
- Users click through to your site

**AEO:**

- Optimizes for AI extraction and presentation
- Focuses on structure and clarity
- Goal: Be cited in AI responses
- Users see your content in AI answers

### The Shift to AI Search

**Statistics:**

- 40% of searches now use AI-powered tools
- ChatGPT has 100M+ weekly users
- Google AI Overviews appear in 15% of searches
- Perplexity handles 500M+ queries/month

**Impact:**

- AI engines extract and present information directly
- Users may never visit your website
- Being cited by AI = massive visibility
- Not being AI-friendly = invisible to AI users

## How AEO Works

### What AI Engines Look For

1. **Direct Answers**

   - Clear, concise responses to questions
   - Facts stated upfront
   - No fluff or filler

2. **Clear Structure**

   - Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
   - Logical organization
   - Easy to scan

3. **Factual Density**

   - High concentration of verifiable facts
   - Specific numbers and data
   - Citations and sources

4. **Context Clarity**

   - Who, what, when, where, why
   - Clear attribution
   - Explicit relationships

5. **Extractability**
   - Bullet points and lists
   - Tables for data
   - FAQ sections
   - Schema markup

### 2. AEO Optimization

Automatically optimizes content for AI engines:

**What Gets Optimized:**

1. **Question-Answer Format**

   - Adds direct answers to common questions
   - Structures content as Q&A where appropriate
   - Leads with answers, then provides details

2. **Clear Hierarchy**

   - Fixes heading structure (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
   - Adds descriptive subheadings
   - Organizes content logically

3. **Factual Statements**

   - Leads paragraphs with key facts
   - Adds specific numbers and data
   - Includes citations and sources

4. **Bullet Points & Lists**

   - Converts paragraphs to lists where appropriate
   - Uses bullets for key information
   - Creates numbered steps for processes

5. **Data Tables**

   - Presents data in structured formats
   - Makes comparisons clear
   - Easy for AI to extract

6. **FAQ Section**

   - Generates relevant questions
   - Provides direct answers
   - Optimized for AI extraction

7. **Schema Markup**
   - Suggests schema.org markup
   - Helps AI understand content structure
   - Improves rich snippet potential

## Best Practices for AEO

‚úÖ **Do:**

- Use bullet points for lists
- Add data tables

‚ùå **Don't:**

### 2. Write for Extraction

‚úÖ **Do:**

- State facts clearly

- Include dates and numbers

‚ùå **Don't:**

### 3. Optimize for Questions

‚úÖ **Do:**

- Add FAQ sections
- Think like a user asking questions

‚ùå **Don't:**

- Use clickbait tactics

### 4. Add Structured Data

‚úÖ **Do:**

- Use schema.org markup

‚ùå **Don't:**

- Rely only on plain text
