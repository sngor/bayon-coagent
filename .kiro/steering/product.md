# Product Overview

Co-agent Marketer is an AI-powered success platform for real estate agents. It helps agents build online authority, track market position, and generate high-quality marketing content.

## Core Features

- **Unified Profile**: Centralized professional information with SEO-friendly schema markup
- **AI Marketing Plan**: Personalized 3-step marketing plans based on brand audit and competitor analysis
- **Brand Audit**: NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency checks and review imports from Zillow
- **Competitive Analysis**: AI-powered competitor discovery and Google keyword ranking tracking
- **Content Engine**: AI content generation for neighborhood guides, social posts, listing descriptions, market updates, video scripts, and blog posts
- **AI Research Agent**: Autonomous deep-dive research on any real estate topic
- **Knowledge Base**: Centralized storage for in-depth research reports
- **Training Hub**: Educational content on local SEO, social media, and content marketing
- **Integrations**: Google Business Profile sync for reviews and business information

## Target Users

Real estate agents looking to enhance their digital presence and streamline content creation.

## Business Model

SaaS platform for real estate professionals with AWS-powered infrastructure for scalability and reliability.

## Key User Flows

1. **Onboarding**: Profile setup → Brand audit → Competitor analysis → Marketing plan generation
2. **Content Creation**: Select content type → Fill form → AI generation → Save to library
3. **Research**: Submit topic → AI research → View report → Save to knowledge base
4. **Integration**: Connect Google Business Profile → Import reviews → Track rankings
