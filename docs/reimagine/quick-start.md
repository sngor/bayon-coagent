Get started with the Reimagine Image Toolkit in 5 minutes! This guide will walk you through your first edit.

1. Log in to your Bayon Coagent account
2. Click **Reimagine** in the main navigation menu
3. You'll see the image upload interface

### Choose a Good Photo

- ‚úÖ A well-lit property photo

**Option A: Drag and Drop**

After accepting:

1. Go to **Edit History** (below the main interface)
2. Find your completed edit
3. Click the **Download** button
4. High-quality image downloads to your computer

## Quick Tips for Success

### Photography Tips

‚úÖ **Do:**

- Use good lighting (natural light is best)
- Shoot from eye level (about 5 feet)
- Turn on all lights for interiors
- Keep camera level and steady
- Use highest quality setting

‚ùå **Don't:**

- Shoot in very dark conditions
- Use extreme angles
- Include too much clutter
- Forget to clean the lens
- Use digital zoom

### Editing Tips

‚úÖ **Do:**

- Start with AI suggestions
- Enhance photos first (before other edits)
- Preview carefully before accepting
- Try regenerate if not satisfied
- Keep edits realistic

‚ùå **Don't:**

- Over-process images
- Skip the preview step
- Rush through decisions
- Ignore AI recommendations
- Forget to download

## Common First-Time Questions

### Q: How long does processing take?

**A:** 15-90 seconds depending on edit type. Enhancement is fastest (15-30s), renovation takes longest (60-90s).

### Q: Can I edit the same image multiple times?

**A:** Yes! After accepting an edit, click "Edit Result" to apply additional edits (chaining).

### Q: What if I don't like the result?

**A:** Click "Regenerate" to adjust parameters and try again, or "Cancel" to discard and try a different edit type.

### Q: How many images can I upload?

**A:** 10 uploads per hour, 20 edit operations per hour. Unlimited storage for your edit history.

### Q: Can I download the original image?

**A:** Yes, your original is always preserved. You can access it from your edit history.

### Q: What if my image is rejected?

**A:** Check that it's under 10MB and in JPEG, PNG, or WebP format. Compress if needed.
