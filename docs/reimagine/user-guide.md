## Getting Started

The Reimagine Image Toolkit is designed specifically for real estate professionals who want to enhance their property photos with AI-powered editing capabilities.

### Accessing the Toolkit

1. Log in to your Bayon Coagent account
2. Navigate to **Reimagine** from the main menu
3. You'll see the image upload interface

### System Requirements

- **Supported Image Formats**: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- **Maximum File Size**: 10MB per image
- **Recommended Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher for best results
- **Internet Connection**: Required for AI processing

## Uploading Images

### How to Upload

There are two ways to upload your property photos:

#### Method 1: Drag and Drop

1. Drag your image file from your computer
2. Drop it onto the upload area
3. The system will validate and preview your image

#### Method 2: Click to Browse

1. Click anywhere in the upload area
2. Select your image file from the file browser
3. Click "Open" to upload

### Upload Validation

The system automatically validates your images:

- ‚úÖ **File Size**: Must be under 10MB
- ‚úÖ **Format**: JPEG, PNG, or WebP only
- ‚úÖ **Content**: Should be a property photo for best results

### What Happens After Upload

1. Your image is securely uploaded to cloud storage
2. AI analyzes your image (takes 5-10 seconds)
3. You receive personalized edit suggestions
4. The image is ready for editing

### Preview Actions

You have three options when previewing:

#### ‚úÖ Accept & Save

## Managing Your Edit History

### Viewing History

Your edit history shows:

- Thumbnail previews
- Edit type and timestamp
- Original and edited versions
- Edit chains (multiple edits on same image)

### Available Actions

- **Download**: Get high-quality edited images
- **Delete**: Remove edits you no longer need
- **Edit Result**: Apply additional edits (chaining)
- **View Details**: See edit parameters used

### Edit Chaining

Apply multiple edits sequentially:

1. Accept your first edit
2. Click "Edit Result" on the completed edit
3. Choose your next edit type
4. The previous result becomes the source

## Best Practices

### Image Quality Tips

1. **Start with high-resolution images** (1920x1080 minimum)
2. **Use well-lit photos** for better AI processing
3. **Keep images under 10MB** by compressing if needed
4. **Shoot in landscape orientation** for property photos

### Editing Workflow

1. **Upload and review AI suggestions** first
2. **Start with enhancement** if image quality is poor
3. **Apply staging or renovation** for empty spaces
4. **Finish with day-to-dusk** for exterior shots
5. **Preview carefully** before accepting

### Professional Results

- **Don't over-edit**: Keep images realistic
- **Match property style**: Choose appropriate furniture/renovation styles
- **Consider your audience**: Edit for your target buyers
- **Test different options**: Use regenerate to try variations

### Rate Limits

To ensure fair usage:

- **Uploads**: 10 per hour
- **Edits**: 20 per hour
- **Storage**: Unlimited edit history

## Troubleshooting

### Upload Issues

**Problem**: "File size exceeds 10MB limit"

- **Solution**: Compress your image using an online tool or photo editor

**Problem**: "Unsupported file format"

- **Solution**: Convert to JPEG, PNG, or WebP format

**Problem**: Upload fails repeatedly

- **Solution**: Check your internet connection and try again

### Processing Issues

**Problem**: Processing takes too long

- **Solution**: Wait up to 60 seconds; complex edits take longer

**Problem**: "Processing failed" error

- **Solution**: Try a different image or adjust parameters

**Problem**: Results don't match expectations

- **Solution**: Use regenerate with different parameters

### Quality Issues

**Problem**: Edited image looks unnatural

- **Solution**: Reduce intensity/strength parameters

**Problem**: Virtual staging furniture doesn't fit

- **Solution**: Ensure room type is selected correctly

**Problem**: Day-to-dusk looks too dark

- **Solution**: Choose "subtle" intensity instead of "dramatic"
